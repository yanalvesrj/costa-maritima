About us

Who are we?

Founded on June 18, 2018 in the state of Santa Catarina, Brazil, the company was created to deal with demands related to the Naval sector in the southern region of the state of Paraná, in Paranaguá. And more precisely, to deal with the demnads of all North of Santa Catarina.

The existing demands in the southern region of the country were a starting point to expand the business to other regions, having Rio de Janeiro and Santa Catarina as strategic regions for Costa Marítima to promote its excellence.
